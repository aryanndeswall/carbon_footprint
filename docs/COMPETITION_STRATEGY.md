# Judge Questions & Answers

---

## Question

How do you calculate emissions?

---

### Answer

All calculations are deterministic and based on versioned emission factors stored in PostgreSQL.

AI never calculates emissions.

---

## Question

Why is AI needed?

---

### Answer

AI improves engagement and personalization.

It explains data and recommends actions.

It is not responsible for calculations.

---

## Question

What prevents hallucinations?

---

### Answer

The Carbon Engine calculates emissions.

Gemini only receives calculated outputs.

All AI responses are validated before display.

---

## Question

Why would users open this daily?

---

### Answer

Daily missions, streaks, community accountability, and personalized coaching create a daily engagement loop.

---

## Question

What is your moat?

---

### Answer

The combination of:

* deterministic sustainability science
* AI coaching
* community accountability
* behavior-change design

creates a defensible product experience.
